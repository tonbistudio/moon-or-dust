### Golden Ages

Triggered by achievements, providing temporary boosts.

**Universal Triggers (all tribes):**
| Achievement | Duration |
|-------------|----------|
| Research 3 techs in 5 turns | 3 turns |
| Capture an enemy capital | 3 turns |
| Found 4th settlement | 3 turns |
| Reach 20 total population | 3 turns |
| Build 2 wonders | 3 turns |
| Earn 3 great people | 3 turns |
| Reach 6 trade routes first | 3 turns |

**Tribe-Specific Triggers:**
| Tribe | Trigger | Duration |
|-------|---------|----------|
| Monkes | Accumulate 500 gold treasury | 4 turns |
| Geckos | Research Era 3 tech first | 4 turns |
| DeGods | Kill 10 enemy units | 4 turns |
| Cets | Complete Era 3 culture first | 4 turns |

**Golden Age Effects:** 
Randomly selected based on era:
Era 1:
- +25% combat strength
- +1 mobility all units
- +20% Alpha yield
- +20% Vibes yield
- +20% production
- +20% Gold income
Era 2:
- +33% defense
- +2 combat strength all units
- +30% Alpha yield
- +30% Vibes yield
- +30% production
- +30% Gold income
Era 3:
- +20% combat strength and +20% defense
- +40% Alpha yield
- +40% Vibes yield
- +40% production
- +40% Gold income
